    It was an interesting book, full of fascinating insights into human relationships.

B篇

  1. identical: adjective 极为相似的
    The tests are identical to those carried out last year.
    The two rooms were virtually identical.

  2. account:
    n.

    1. 账户，户头
      I’ve opened an account with a building society.
      She paid the cheque into her account.[UK]= She deposited the check in her account.[US]
      I need to draw some money out of my account.
    2. 报道；报告；记述；描述
      She gave a thrilling account of her life in the jungle.
      He kept a detailed account of the suspect’s movements.
      • by/from all accounts 据各方面所说
        By all accounts, San Francisco is a city that’s easy to fall in love with.
      • by your own account:据…自己所说
        By his own account, he’s quite wealthy.
    3. 因为 on account of sth
      He doesn’t drink alcohol on account of his poor health.
    4. 赊销账；赊欠账；赊购
      Could you please pay/settle your account in full (= give us all the money you owe us)?
      Could you put it on/charge it to my account (= can I pay for it later), please?
      Do you have an account at this store/with us, madam?
    5. 顾客
    6. 重要:It’s of no account to me whether he comes or not.

    v.

    1. 认为是；视为
      She was accounted a genius.
      • account for sth Students 占据 account for the vast majority of our customers.
      • account (to sb) for sth 说明 Can you account for your absence last Friday?
  3. -proof 防……的 a waterproof/wind-proof jacket
  4. -prone 较常碰到…问题的 accident-prone
  5. feature
    v. 以…为特色；给…以显著的地位
    This week’s broadcast features a report on victims of domestic violence.
    n.
    1. 特色，特征，特点 Our latest model of phone has several new features.
    2. （建筑物的）外部特征；地形 a geographical feature
    3. 面容的一部分；面貌 He has wonderful strong <>features. //regular features 五官端正
    4. （报纸或杂志的）特写；（电视或广播的）专题节目 a feature on sth.
    5. 电影正片

C篇

  1. on the go
    • 忙个不停的，特别忙的 I’ve been on the go all day, and I’m really tired.
    • 正在生产中，处于生产过程中 Did you know that she’s got a new book on the go (= being written)?

D篇

  1. draw on 利用,借鉴
    His novels draw heavily on his childhood.

语填

  1. highlight
    v. 使引起注意，强调:
    The report highlights the need for improved safety.
    The spelling mistakes in the text had been highlighted in green.
    n. 最精彩的部分
    Highlights of the match will be shown after the news. //挑染的头发
