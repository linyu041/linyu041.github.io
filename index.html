厦门市质检整理

发表于 2019-06-21 | 更新于 2019-06-22 | 分类于 英语试卷整理

A篇

  1. For some animals…;For others…;For still others…
  2. insight: noun [ C or U ] 洞察力,深刻见解
    It was an interesting book, full of fascinating insights into human relationships.

  3. cast
    n.[C]

    1. （一部电影、戏剧或演出的）全体演员 Part of the movie’s success lies in the strength of the supporting cast (配角).
    2. 铸件，铸造品
    3. 石膏绷带，石膏夹
      in a cast 打着石膏

    v.

    1. 分配…扮演（某角色)
      He was often cast as the villain. 他经常演反面角色
      In her latest movie she was cast against type (改变戏路)
    2. B篇
  1. identical: adjective 极为相似的
    The tests are identical to those carried out last year.
    The two rooms were virtually identical.

  2. account:
    n.

    1. 账户，户头
      I’ve opened an account with a building society.
      She paid the cheque into her account.[UK]= She deposited the check in her account.[US]
      I need to draw some money out of my account.
    2. 报道；报告；记述；描述
      She gave a thrilling account of her life in the jungle.
      He kept a detailed account of the suspect’s movements.
      • by/from all accounts 据各方面所说
        By all accounts, San Francisco is a city that’s easy to fall in love with.
      • by your own account:据…自己所说
        By his own account, he’s quite wealthy.
    3. 因为 on account of sth
      He doesn’t drink alcohol on account of his poor health.
    4. 赊销账；赊欠账；赊购
      Could you please pay/settle your account in full (= give us all the money you owe us)?
      Could you put it on/charge it to my account (= can I pay for it later), please?
      Do you have an account at this store/with us, madam?
    5. 顾客
    6. 重要:It’s of no account to me whether he comes or not.

    v.

    1. 认为是；视为
      She was accounted a genius.
      • account for sth Students 占据 account for the vast majority of our customers.
      • account (to sb) for sth 说明 Can you account for your absence last Friday?
  3. -proof 防……的 a waterproof/wind-proof jacket
  4. -prone 较常碰到…问题的 accident-prone
  5. feature
    v. 以…为特色；给…以显著的地位
    This week’s broadcast features a report on victims of domestic violence.
    n.
    1. 特色，特征，特点 Our latest model of phone has several new features.
    2. （建筑物的）外部特征；地形 a geographical feature
    3. 面容的一部分；面貌 He has wonderful strong <>features. //regular features 五官端正
    4. （报纸或杂志的）特写；（电视或广播的）专题节目 a feature on sth.
    5. 电影正片

C篇

  1. on the go
    • 忙个不停的，特别忙的 I’ve been on the go all day, and I’m really tired.
    • 正在生产中，处于生产过程中 Did you know that she’s got a new book on the go (= being written)?

D篇

  1. draw on 利用,借鉴
    His novels draw heavily on his childhood.

语填

  1. highlight
    v. 使引起注意，强调:
    The report highlights the need for improved safety.
    The spelling mistakes in the text had been highlighted in green.
    n. 最精彩的部分
    Highlights of the match will be shown after the news. //挑染的头发
  2. close&closely一类词的辨析

M8U3小结

发表于 2019-06-03 | 分类于 英语素材 ， 英语小结

  • patent
    n. 专利
    v. 申请专利: If you don’t patent your invention, other people may make all the profit out of it.
    adj. 显然
    take out/file a patent on sth. 申请某物专利
    when does the patent expire? 专利到期
    be protected by a patent

  • call up
    1.给…打电话(call sb. up):My dad called me up to tell me the good news.
    2.征召…入伍；征调…入队（尤指加入国家队)(call sb. up):He was called up when the war began.
    3.（在电脑屏幕上）显示，调出（信息）(call sth. up):You can use the search facility to call up all the occurrences of a particular word in a document.
    关于call更多词语,详见:call

  • distinguish
    distinguish between A and B:He’s colour-blind and can’t distinguish (the difference) between red and green easily.
    distinguish A from B:They look so similar it’s often difficult to distinguish one from the other.
    distinguish oneself: 使自己出类拔萃，表现突出:He distinguished himself as a writer at a very early age.

  • mercy n.
    1.慈悲，怜悯[U]:
    They begged for mercy.
    The gunmen showed no mercy, killing innocent men and women.
    2.恩惠[S]:
    After months of suffering, his death was a mercy.
    a mercy mission 救援任务

  • set about 开始做,开始处理
    +Ving: I have no idea how to set about changing a tyre(轮胎) on a car.

  • set out （怀着特定目的）开始，着手;出发,启程
    She set out with the aim of becoming the youngest ever winner of the championship.
    They set out to discover a cure for cancer.
  • abrupt
    1.突然的；意外的
    an abrupt change/movement 突发情况
    2.（言语）生硬的；鲁莽的；唐突的
    an abrupt manner 唐突的举止
    He is sometimes very abrupt with clients.

  • seize
    1.夺过,抓住
    He seized the chance/opportunity of a free flight with both hands (= with eagerness or enthusiasm).
    I seized his arm.
    2.夺取；攫取；抓获
    Political instability helped the army to seize power.
    Troops yesterday seized control of the broadcasting station.
    seized ten soldiers to use as hostages.
    3.没收
    Customs officers at Heathrow have seized 60 kilos of heroin.
    4.（强烈情感或剧痛）突然侵袭 [usually passive]
    I was suddenly seized by/with a feeling of great insecurity and loneliness.

    seize on/upon sth. 抓住,大加利用
    The story was seized on by the tabloid press, who printed it under huge headlines.

  • criterion
    set up/establish/lay down criterion
    satisfy/meet criterion
  • file v.
    1.把…归档 file these reports
    2.（尤指在法庭）提起（诉讼）: The police filed charges against the two suspects.
    3.发送（报道）Our foreign correspondent filed this report earlier today.
    4.排成纵队行进 The visitors filed through the entrance to the ticket offices.
    5.锉平；锉光滑；锉去
    File (down) the sharp edges.
    The surface had been filed smooth.
    She filed her nails.
